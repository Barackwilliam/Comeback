Mount Meru stands as Tanzania's magnificent second-highest peak and Africa's fifth highest mountain. Often overshadowed by its famous neighbor Kilimanjaro, Mount Meru offers a more intimate and equally spectacular climbing experience with breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro from its summit.

The 4-day Momela Route represents the optimal balance between challenge and enjoyment. This extended itinerary provides better acclimatization, increased summit success rates (98%), and more time to appreciate the diverse ecosystems you'll traverse—from lush montane rainforest teeming with wildlife to alpine deserts and the dramatic volcanic summit crater.

This expedition serves as excellent preparation for Kilimanjaro or as a spectacular standalone achievement. With expert guides, comfortable mountain huts, and carefully planned acclimatization, we ensure your safety and success on this unforgettable journey to Socialist Peak (4,566m).

4-Day Climbing Itinerary

Your optimal journey to the summit with proper acclimatization

DAY 1

ARUSHA → MOMELLA GATE → MIRIAKAMBA HUTS

Your adventure begins with pickup from Arusha and a scenic drive to Arusha National Park's Momella Gate. After park formalities and meeting your expert mountain crew, you'll start ascending through lush montane rainforest.

This 5-6 hour hike serves as a gentle introduction, offering opportunities to spot black and white colobus monkeys, giraffes, and buffalo. You'll spend your first night at Miriakamba Huts (2,514m), featuring solar lighting and comfortable bedding.

DAY 2

MIRIAKAMBA HUTS → SADDLE HUTS

Today's trek ascends through the heath and moorland zone, leaving the forest behind for open terrain with giant heather and lobelia plants. The trail follows the crater rim, offering spectacular views of the Meru Crater and the "Ash Tray" volcanic cone.

An optional side trip to Little Mount Meru (3,820m) provides panoramic views of Arusha and surrounding plains. You'll overnight at Saddle Huts (3,570m), strategically positioned for your summit attempt with extra time for acclimatization.

DAY 3

SUMMIT ASCENT → RETURN TO MIRIAKAMBA

Summit day begins around midnight with hot tea and snacks. Ascend by headlamp through volcanic terrain, passing Rhino Point and Cobra Point before reaching Socialist Peak (4,566m) for sunrise.

The pre-dawn effort rewards you with breathtaking views—Kilimanjaro glowing in the east, the Meru Crater below, and African plains stretching to the horizon. After celebrating your achievement, descend to Miriakamba Huts for a well-earned rest and recovery night.

Key Advantage: Extra recovery night improves safety and comfort
