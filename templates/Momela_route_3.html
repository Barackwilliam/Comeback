Mount Meru Climbing Expedition

Mount Meru stands majestically as Tanzania's second highest peak and Africa's fifth highest mountain. While often overshadowed by its famous neighbor Kilimanjaro, Mount Meru offers an equally spectacular and more intimate climbing experience with breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro from its summit.

The Momela Route 3-day ascent is perfect for those seeking a challenging yet achievable mountain adventure. You'll hike through diverse ecological zones—from lush rainforest teeming with wildlife to alpine deserts and finally the volcanic summit crater. Sunrise from Socialist Peak offers one of Africa's most magnificent vistas, with Kilimanjaro dominating the eastern horizon.

This expedition serves as excellent preparation for Kilimanjaro or as a spectacular standalone achievement. With expert guides, comfortable mountain huts, and carefully planned acclimatization, we ensure your safety and success on this unforgettable journey.

Climbing Itinerary

Your step-by-step journey to the summit

DAY 1

ARUSHA → MOMELLA GATE → MIRIAKAMBA HUTS

Your adventure begins with pickup from Arusha accommodations and a scenic drive to Arusha National Park's Momella Gate. After completing park formalities and meeting your mountain crew, you'll start your ascent through lush montane rainforest.

This initial 5-6 hour hike serves as a gentle introduction, offering opportunities to spot black and white colobus monkeys, giraffes, and buffalo. You'll spend your first night at Miriakamba Huts (2,514m), modern facilities with solar lighting and comfortable bedding.

Hiking Time: 5-6 hours | Altitude Gain: 1,000m

DAY 2

MIRIAKAMBA HUTS → SADDLE HUTS

Today's trek ascends steeply through the heath and moorland zone, leaving the forest behind for more open terrain with giant heather and lobelia plants. The trail follows the crater rim, offering spectacular views of the Meru Crater and the "Ash Tray" volcanic cone.

For those with extra energy, an optional side trip to Little Mount Meru (3,820m) provides panoramic views of Arusha and the surrounding plains. You'll overnight at Saddle Huts (3,570m), strategically positioned for your summit attempt.

Pro Tip: Hydrate well and prepare your summit gear before an early bedtime.

Hiking Time: 4-5 hours | Altitude Gain: 1,056m

DAY 3

SUMMIT ASCENT → ARUSHA

Summit day begins around midnight with hot tea and a light snack. You'll ascend by headlamp through volcanic terrain, passing Rhino Point and Cobra Point before reaching Socialist Peak (4,566m) for sunrise.

The pre-dawn effort rewards you with breathtaking views—Kilimanjaro glowing in the east, the Meru Crater below, and the African plains stretching to the horizon. After celebrating your achievement, you'll descend to Momella Gate where our vehicle awaits to return you to Arusha, arriving by late afternoon.

Summit Experience: Witness one of Africa's most spectacular sunrises.

Hiking Time: 10-12 hours | Summit: 4,566m
